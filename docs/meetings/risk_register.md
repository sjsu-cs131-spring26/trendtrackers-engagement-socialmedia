# Sprint 3 – Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Limited dataset sample size (10K) | Trends may not fully represent overall activity | Acknowledge sampling limitation and compare runs across different days |
| Missing or inconsistent metadata | Incorrect frequency calculations | Perform validation checks before analysis |
| Uneven language distribution | Bias toward dominant language content | Include language frequency check and restrict analysis to English entries |

---

## Monitoring Approach
Risks will be reviewed during weekly sprint check-ins and adjustments will be made if data inconsistencies are observed.
